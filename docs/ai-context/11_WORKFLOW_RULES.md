# Workflow Rules

## Baseline Workflow Principles

- Greenfield first: minimum dataset can go live; full historical migration is not prerequisite.
- Batch-first operation: data intake, validation, import, certificate updates, inspection assignment, document tagging, evidence selection, and reviewer assignment must support batch operation.
- Progressive data enrichment: data evolves from minimum registry to compliance tracking, inspection/certification readiness, RBI readiness, and advanced assessment.
- Risk-prioritized workflow: not every equipment enters full technical workflow; route by criticality, statutory obligation, due status, risk indicator, service scope, and client need.
- Approval by exception: apply layered approval only where technical/compliance/risk/certification/evidence export/configuration baseline impact warrants it.
- Role-based work queue: reviewers and approvers work from prioritized queues by role, priority, due date, risk level, status, module, and site.
- Document reuse: documents/evidence are reusable linked objects.
- Dashboard-to-action: dashboards must link to action lists.
- Human final decision: platform outputs are draft/preliminary until authorized review/approval.

## Required Operational Workflows

- Project/site onboarding and configuration baseline.
- Batch data intake and asset registry baseline.
- Asset scoping and criticality routing.
- Compliance baseline and obligation register.
- Statutory inspection tracking and workpack trigger.
- Inspection execution and findings/recommendations.
- Certification support and renewal readiness.
- RBI workflow and risk review.
- Risk register and technical verification support.
- Anomaly/action close-out.
- Document repository and evidence pack builder.
- Reviewer work queue and approval by exception.
- Dashboard, reporting, and management action.
- IOW/MOC/revalidation trigger placeholder.
- Helpdesk, bug log, and support escalation.
- Gate review, UAT, and scale-up governance.

## High-Volume Operating Rules

- Use templates and clone configuration for similar sites.
- Use validation queue and data quality flags for imports.
- Use digest, threshold, escalation rule, grouping, saved views, and suppression to avoid alert fatigue.
- Track workload metrics such as pending items, review time, error rate, data gap count, and actions per role during pilot.
