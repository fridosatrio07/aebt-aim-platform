# AIM Platform Project Context

## Project Identity

Project name: AIM Platform - Assets Integrity Management Platform SBU AEBT PT SUCOFINDO.

AIM Platform is a web-based managed platform for asset integrity management. The source documents position it as a workflow, data, evidence, compliance support, audit trail, reporting, and decision-support platform for SBU AEBT services. It is intended to turn inspection, certification, integrity, and risk information that is often stored in PDFs, spreadsheets, folders, and project archives into governed operational data.

## Target Users and Sectors

Target sectors include oil and gas, geothermal, petrochemical, chemical manufacturing, high-risk industrial facilities, and other facilities that require technical inspection, certification support, risk engineering, technical verification, or asset integrity management.

Target users include internal PT SUCOFINDO/SBU AEBT users, client/asset-owner users, governance reviewers, and external parties where applicable. Source roles include platform administrator, system administrator, inspector, RBI engineer, certification team, technical specialist/SME, data analyst/reporting analyst, document controller, Q&C reviewer, legal reviewer, management reviewer, sales/account manager, helpdesk/customer support, Project Owner, client administrator, asset owner representative, site/facility manager, inspection/maintenance/integrity engineer, process/mechanical/electrical/civil engineer, HSE/compliance user, certification/document controller, management user, auditor/viewer, regulator, certification body, subcontractor/NDT vendor, external auditor, and client systems such as CMMS/EAM/ERP.

## Core Modules

- Asset registry and asset hierarchy.
- Statutory inspection tracking.
- Certification support.
- RBI workflow and controlled integrity assessment support.
- Risk register.
- Anomaly and action tracking.
- Document repository.
- Evidence pack builder and evidence reuse.
- Audit trail, change log, access log, approval log, and export log.
- Compliance dashboard, reporting, and analytics support.
- Tenant, project, site, user, role, permission, and approval workflow administration.
- Regulatory standard library and compliance matrix traceability.
- Data import mapping, validation queue, and data quality control.
- My Work / Action Inbox / reviewer work queue.

## Operating Scale

The platform must support high-volume operation for sites or facilities with hundreds to thousands of equipment. Future implementations must prefer batch import, saved views, filtered action lists, validation queues, reviewer work queues, notification digests, dashboard-to-action links, and evidence reuse instead of forcing asset-by-asset manual work.

## Decision Boundary

AIM Platform must not automatically make final technical, certification, legal, RBI, RLA, FFS, fit-for-operation, inspection interval extension, risk acceptance, or asset safety decisions. Technical outputs, extraction results, due-date calculations, completeness checks, draft recommendations, helper calculations, and preliminary risk rankings remain draft/preliminary until reviewed and approved by authorized personnel.
