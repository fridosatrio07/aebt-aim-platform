# Domain Rules and Guardrails

## Allowed Platform Outputs

AIM Platform may generate or support:

- Alerts and reminders.
- Due date calculations.
- Completeness checks.
- Data gap flags and validation issues.
- Draft recommendations.
- Draft/helper calculations.
- Preliminary risk rankings.
- Draft RBI outputs.
- Evidence packs.
- Dashboard indicators and action lists.
- Extraction results and import staging records.

## Prohibited Automatic Final Decisions

The platform must not automatically declare, approve, issue, or finalize:

- Asset safe status.
- Fit for operation.
- Layak operasi.
- Certification-ready final status.
- Certificate or PLO issuance.
- Final RBI, RLA, or FFS result.
- Repair, alteration, rerating, or interval extension approval.
- Inspection interval extension.
- Risk acceptance criteria change.
- Final legal interpretation.
- Final compliance interpretation.
- Final asset safety or technical suitability decision.

## Review and Approval Boundary

All technical outputs remain draft/preliminary until reviewed and approved by authorized personnel. Applicable reviewers may include engineer, inspector, SME, Q&C, Legal, certification body, regulator, client authority, or other authorized personnel based on context.

## Regulatory and Standard Interpretation

Regulatory/standard interpretation must be reviewed by Legal, Q&C, engineer, inspector, and SME as applicable. Agents must not invent formulas, clauses, compliance mappings, RBI methodology details, RLA/FFS rules, certification rules, or legal conclusions.
