# Product Scope MVP+

## Source-Backed MVP+ Definition

MVP+ is the controlled baseline for a limited commercial pilot. It must provide enough workflow, data control, evidence handling, review, approval, and reporting support to operate AIM Platform for selected sites/facilities without requiring full historical migration before go-live.

## In-Scope MVP+ Modules

- Tenant, client, project, site, user, role, permission, and approval workflow.
- Asset registry using ISO 14224-based taxonomy principles with configurable hierarchy up to 9 levels.
- Statutory inspection tracking for due dates, reminders, overdue flag, inspection calendar, and due-overdue reporting.
- Certification support for certificate register, renewal tracker, submission log, approval log, document checklist, and evidence readiness.
- Controlled RBI workflow from assessment basis to review and approval, with draft/preliminary outputs only.
- Risk register for RBI, risk engineering, and technical verification support.
- Document repository with metadata, versioning, controlled access, and linkage to asset/equipment, inspection, certificate, risk, anomaly, and evidence.
- Evidence pack builder for audit, certification support, RBI review, inspection workpack, recommendation close-out, and compliance export.
- Audit trail, change log, approval log, access log, and report export log.
- Compliance dashboard for inspection due/overdue, certificate due/expired, high-risk asset, open anomaly, open recommendation, missing evidence, and review status.
- Regulatory standard library and compliance matrix traceability.
- Data import mapping for greenfield onboarding.
- Helpdesk flow, bug log, basic training/SOP support, and support escalation path before pilot.

## Addendum MVP+ Items

- Workload reduction for high-volume operation.
- Assisted data intake, including import mapping and duplicate/error handling.
- Validation queue and data quality gates.
- Action-based navigation through My Work, Action Inbox, filtered action lists, and dashboard-to-action drill-down.
- Evidence reuse across modules without repeated upload.
- Reviewer work queue and approval by exception.
- Data quality control for imported, extracted, manually entered, historical, or assumed technical data.
- Notification digest and escalation grouping to avoid alert fatigue.

## Explicitly Out of Scope for MVP+

- Automatic certificate/PLO issuance or final certification decision.
- Automatic declaration that an asset is safe, fit for operation, layak operasi, or final-compliant.
- Automatic final approval of RBI, RLA, FFS, repair, alteration, rerating, inspection interval extension, or risk acceptance criteria changes.
- Final legal interpretation of regulations or standards.
- Formal ISO 27001 or ISO 55001 certification.
- AI/ML recommendation engine that creates technical decisions without SME validation.
- Full integration with all client systems, except limited pilot integrations explicitly approved.
- Full historical data migration as a go-live prerequisite.

## Scale-Up Items Requiring Approval

- Expanded regulatory obligation library beyond MVP+ pilot scope.
- Advanced RBI analytics, optimization, and engineering analytics.
- FFS/RLA workflow, anomaly disposition expansion, pipeline/tank/PRD integrity modules, process safety KPI, environmental reporting, lender/ESG evidence tagging.
- Enterprise integration/API layer with document management, ERP, CMMS, EAM, or client reporting pipelines.
- Multi-site dashboard, executive dashboard, portfolio risk dashboard, client-level compliance dashboard, and recurring service performance dashboard.
- ISO 27001/ISO 55001 formal readiness programs.
- AI/ML readiness after data quality, governance, security control, and SME validation mechanisms are approved.
